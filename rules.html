📜 Server Rules

I. RESPECT & CONDUCT

Treat all players with respect at all times.
Harassment, insults, discrimination, or toxicity are not allowed.
Keep disagreements civil and in-character when possible.

II. CLEAN & APPROPRIATE CHAT

No hate speech, slurs, or offensive language.
No sexual content or implications, especially involving minors.
Political topics, personal drama, and controversial subjects must stay out of public chat.

III. NO SPAM OR ADVERTISING

Do not flood chat with messages, symbols, or noise.
No advertising in public chat or private messages without staff approval.
Consult an Admin through tickets before promoting anything.

IV. PRIVACY & SAFETY

Do not share personal information, private messages, or identities without permission.
Respect everyone’s privacy.

V. VOICE CHAT CONDUCT

No screaming, mic abuse, or disruptive noises.
Keep voice chat calm and respectful.

VI. NAMES, SKINS & PROFILES

Usernames, skins, avatars, and statuses must be appropriate.
No impersonating staff or other players.

VII. USE COMMON SENSE

Not every rule can be listed.
If something feels wrong or unfair, do not do it.

VIII. CIV EVENT RULES

No alliances, recruiting, peace treaties, bounties, or wars before events officially begin.
Lore actions should happen during the event.
Do not use pre-made large Discord servers to mass recruit players.
Recruiting should be done in-character and within the lore.

IX. ZERO TOLERANCE BEHAVIOR

Any predatory, sexual, or highly inappropriate behavior will result in immediate removal with no warnings.

⚖️ Enforcement

Rule breaking usually follows escalation:
Warning → Mute → Ban

Certain actions (impersonation, predatory behavior, cheating) may result in instant bans.

Carry yourself responsibly.
Use common sense.
