📜 Server Rules

I. RESPECT & CONDUCT

Treat all players with respect at all times.

No harassment, insults, discrimination, or toxicity.

Keep disagreements civil and in-character when possible.

II. CLEAN & APPROPRIATE CHAT

No hate speech, slurs, or offensive language.

No sexual content or implications.

No political or controversial topics in public chat.

III. NO SPAM OR ADVERTISING

No flooding chat or spam.

No advertising without staff approval.

Contact Admin before promoting anything.

IV. PRIVACY & SAFETY

Do not share personal information.

Respect everyone’s privacy.

V. VOICE CHAT CONDUCT

No screaming or mic abuse.

Keep voice chat respectful.

VI. NAMES, SKINS & PROFILES

Keep usernames and profiles appropriate.

No impersonation.

VII. USE COMMON SENSE

If something feels wrong, don’t do it.

VIII. CIV EVENT RULES

No alliances or wars before event start.

Lore actions must happen during event.

No mass recruiting via Discord servers.

Recruit in-character only.

IX. ZERO TOLERANCE

Any predatory or highly inappropriate behavior = instant ban.

⚠️ Rule breaking may result in:

Warning → Mute → Ban

Severe actions may result in instant bans.

Use common sense. Carry yourself responsibly.
