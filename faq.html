❓ FAQ
How long do applications take?
▼
Applications are usually reviewed within 1–3 days depending on volume.
Can I reapply?
▼
Yes. If you are not accepted, you can reapply in the next application wave.
Do I need a team?
▼
No. Teams and alliances are formed during the event itself.
How will I know if I got accepted?
▼
You will be contacted through Discord if you are accepted.
